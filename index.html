Departments

At Lusenac Hospital, our departments are structured to deliver efficient, focused care across a range of essential health services. Whether you’re here for a routine consultation or urgent care, each department is staffed with experienced professionals ready to support your health.

General Medicine Pediatrics OB/GYN Laboratory Pharmacy

General Medicine

Our General Medicine department provides diagnosis and treatment for everyday health concerns — from infections and fevers to long-term conditions like diabetes and hypertension.

Medical Consultations

Expert advice and care for common illnesses and ongoing health issues.

Chronic Disease Care

Management plans for hypertension, diabetes, and other long-term conditions.

Pediatrics

We provide gentle and expert medical care for children from birth through adolescence, in a child-friendly environment.

Child Wellness Checks

Routine checkups to ensure your child is developing healthily and happily.

Immunizations

Up-to-date vaccinations for babies and children according to Ghana Health Service guidelines.

Obstetrics & Gynecology

We support women at every stage — from family planning and fertility care to pregnancy monitoring and hormonal health.

Antenatal Care

Safe and supportive pregnancy checkups with experienced professionals.

Women's Health

Treatment for menstrual, hormonal, and gynecological conditions.

Laboratory Services

Our in-house lab provides fast and accurate diagnostics to support timely treatment decisions.

Blood & Urine Tests

Reliable testing for infections, sugar levels, malaria, and more.

Health Screenings

Preventive tests for high-risk patients and routine health monitoring.

Pharmacy

Get your prescriptions filled quickly and reliably right inside our hospital — no extra trips needed.

Prescription Medications

Immediate access to doctor-prescribed drugs and essential treatments.

Medication Support

Guidance on proper use, dosage, and possible side effects from our trained team.
